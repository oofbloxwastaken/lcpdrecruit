Starting Salary Now $170/hour
After 3 months of service, salary reaches $190/hour. Full payment for educational classes, medical, dental, and vision. Coverage. Up to $5,000 in Housing Assistance (upon qualification).

PRESS RELEASE APRIL 3, 1996

Police Officers overwhelmingly approved a contract that gives them a 11.1% raise over three months of service. Members of the PBA approved the contract by 99.4%.

Under the agreement, the next Academy class will have a starting base salary of $170 an hour and goes to $170 after graduation from the probation phase. When adding holiday pay and overtime, the benefits of being an officer with The Liberty City Police Department are larger than many across the nation!

The base pay will increase after 3 months to $190 an hour. Additionally, longevity pay can increase this even further! A Police Officer with Longevity Level 3 will earn $230 an hour before overtime.
